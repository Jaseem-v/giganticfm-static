Energy Audits

Gigantic Facility Management's Energy Audits offer a detailed and comprehensive assessment of your energy consumption patterns and efficiency. Our expert team conducts thorough inspections of your facilities, identifying areas of energy wastage and potential opportunities for improvement. Through data analysis and detailed reports, we provide valuable insights and recommendations to help you optimize energy usage, reduce costs, and enhance sustainability. With Gigantic Facility Management's Energy Audits, businesses can make informed decisions to achieve significant energy savings and contribute to a greener and more sustainable future.

We conduct energy audits in four levels:

Level 0 – Benchmarking: Assessment of the building’s energy cost and efficiency by conducting a brief on-site survey of the building

Level 1 – Walk-through audit: Preliminary analysis is made to assess the energy efficiency, and provide a list of energy conservation measures to be adopted

Level 2 – Detailed energy audit: A Comprehensive study on existing installation in the facility to identify and resolve the defects. This level can involve advanced techniques and computer-based simulation tools to carry out precise evaluation.

Level 3 – Investment-Grade audit: Detailed analysis and engineering study to incorporate modifications in the existing system focusing more on Energy Conservation Measures.

Measurement & Verification

Gigantic Facility Management's Energy Management includes Measurement & Verification (M&V) services, a crucial aspect of our commitment to data-driven energy efficiency. M&V involves the accurate measurement and analysis of energy consumption before and after implementing energy-saving initiatives. Our expert team utilizes advanced monitoring and data analysis tools to quantify the impact of energy efficiency projects. Through comprehensive M&V, we verify the actual energy savings achieved, ensuring that your investments in energy-saving measures yield the expected results.

By employing rigorous measurement and verification techniques, businesses can gain confidence in the effectiveness of their energy efficiency efforts. The data-driven approach enables informed decision-making, identifying successful strategies and areas for further improvement. With Gigantic Facility Management's Measurement & Verification services, businesses can achieve greater transparency in energy consumption and realize the full potential of energy efficiency initiatives, contributing to a more sustainable and cost-effective operation.

We make sincere efforts to verify whether the installed equipment is able to produce expected cost savings. M&V services consist of the following:

Meter installation and maintenance

Gathering and screening of data

Developing a computation method and acceptable estimates

Computations with measured data

Reporting and verification
