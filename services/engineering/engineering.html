MEP Drafting

Gigantic Facility Management provides expert MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) Drafting services, assisting businesses with accurate and detailed technical drawings for their construction and engineering projects. Our experienced drafters use advanced software to create precise MEP layouts and plans, ensuring that all systems are meticulously coordinated and integrated.

With our MEP Drafting services, businesses can benefit from well-organized and error-free drawings, minimizing construction delays and rework. We prioritize clarity and compliance with industry standards, enabling seamless communication between architects, engineers, and contractors throughout the project lifecycle. Our team pays special attention to the specific requirements of each project, ensuring that MEP drawings reflect the unique needs and objectives of the facility. From electrical layouts to plumbing systems and HVAC ducting, our drafting solutions cover every aspect of MEP design.

Aided with the insight and knowledge gained over experience, we are equipped to handle projects of all sizes and complexities. We can produce MEP drawings for a wide range of projects such as high-rise residential towers, shopping malls, medical laboratories, power plants, etc. Apart from 2D drafting using AutoCAD MEP, we have experience in MEP 3D modeling and clash coordination with the help of BIM (Building Information Modelling) software tools.

Our team offer superior quality drafting services with unparalleled precision that no one can match. Our company consists of experienced architects, designers, and draftsmen who can help you deliver your building projects successfully.
